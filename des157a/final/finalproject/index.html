Focus Game

Instructions

Lets see how focused you really are! Try to get as many points as you can in 30 seconds!

  • There will be two buttons.
  • To score points, click on the button that the prompt provides!
  • The prompt will be written on a button. For example, when 'left', is written, click on the left button!
  • After level 10, the prompt may be written in red, which means you have to click the opposite button of what is written! For example, if left is written, then you have to click the right tile.

Good luck and hope you have fun!
